Corporate Governance

We have a Corporate Governance policy of our own which is strictly followed by each and every person associated with Research Mart Services Private Limited. The policy can be understand:

  • Preserving the rights, interests and concerns of the employees as well as the clients.
  • Taking care of clients’ investment with us and ensuring them best returns.
  • Ensuring that best services are rendered to the clients. Continuously improving communication and reporting structure in the organization.
  • Effectively utilizing organization’s resources based on budgeting, financial and other mechanisms.
  • We strictly follow the ethical business policies while growing our business.
  • We maintain the information on a website which provides in depth knowledge of our services in accurate, efficient and most accessible way.
  • Improve communication and relationship with the clients and keep it as healthy as possible.
